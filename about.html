Who am I?

I am a digital designer mainly focused on design strategy, web design, logo design, and branding. But also sometimes working with photo and video editing, UX testing. I studied my bachelor's degree in the UK at Sheffield Hallam University. In addition, I have my master's diploma from Scuola Politecnica di Design. My education has set up my mind as a designer and has built me as a person with a creative eye who can find solutions by mixing the client’s needs and the latest design trends. As a designer, I love to build a project from the research stage to the final result. I am enjoying a lot to see how a project is developing and building. 

At the moment I am working at my own design studio in Finland as a digital art director. My role as a digital art director and the owner of the studio is searching for solutions for different types of client’s needs, develop design ideas for small and big businesses.
