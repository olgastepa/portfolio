Web design for cats hotel

This project is about to design a website (desktop & responsive options) for a cat's hotel in Finland. The whole website supposes to present information about the hotel and should have online booking.

The idea

The idea of this web design project is to make a website with will have a fresh look and feel, will be attractive with colors and will follow the latest digital design trends.

Logo

The logo is created in the most simple way of showing the brand. There is a cat’s shape on the white circle and the title of the company is under the circle.

Home page

The home page is designed with the latest web design trends. The intro section on each page has full width and heigh picture with the navigation on the tip and social media links on the right.

About us

About us page is designed in a similar way as the home page. There is a picture with a title and under it, there is information about the company.

Services page

The services page also has a big picture and the title. Under it, there is a section where clients can choose a service and book it online.

Contact page

Contact us page has all the contact links, all of them are placed in a row and have icons.

About us responsive

About us responsive has all the information as in the desktop but it is placed in columns.

Services page responsive

The services page is also displayed in a column.

Services page responsive

The services page is in a column. All the information is presented clearly and it is easy to navigate.
