Web design for design studio

This is a project about branding, designing & developing a website, creating social media content.

The idea

The idea is to build a new brand for a digital design studio based in Finland. The goal of the project is to design a brand using a simple interface, minimalistic animation, and identical design.

Home page

Home page is created with slideshow explaining what the company is about. This page has half circle which is the part of the brand and has a title with the animation on the top.

Products page

Services page is about to demonstrate what the company does. It is presented very simple with icons, titles, and descriptions. It is very basic but at the same time gives elegant and Scandinavian simplicity feeling.

Contact page

This is the online form. It is designed in Scandinavian design with simple look.

Contact page

Contact page designed with icons and contact information. All the links are presented on the half-circle on the background.

Menu page

Menu mobile is a hamburger menu. It is precise, user-friendly and gives only necessary information.

Contact page responsive

Responsive contact page is displayed in the same way as for the desktop version.
