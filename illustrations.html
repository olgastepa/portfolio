Illustrations collections

Here are some illustrations which were done as a part of different tasks from clients. There are a postcard collection and some Christmas time postcards. This project is about illustrations. There are two different collections. One collection is about historical buildings in different countries. The other one is a Christmas collection.

The idea

The goal of the first collection is to make a collection showing different countries by illustrating the main building of a city. Also, mix the historical building with the national colors of a country.

The idea of the second collection is to illustrate Christmas trees and give a special Christmas mood.
